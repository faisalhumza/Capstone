Our project is called CrimaVision

The computer engineering capstone project involves a Raspberry Pi 4 and webcam for license plate and facial recognition, with a webpage hosted on the Pi 4 displaying camera feeds and a map location GUI with scan pins. The facial recognition component identifies individuals and their criminal status, while the license plate component displays car information and flags stolen vehicles.
